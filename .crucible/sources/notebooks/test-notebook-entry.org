#+TITLE: Experiment: Measuring Activation Energy of CO Oxidation
#+DATE: 2026-04-01

* Experiment Setup

We prepared a Pt(111) surface and measured CO oxidation rates at
temperatures ranging from 300K to 600K.

* Results

The Arrhenius plot shows a linear relationship with an activation
energy of 1.05 eV, consistent with literature values citep:jones2023.

* Conclusions

The activation energy is in good agreement with DFT predictions
from our earlier calculations cite:smith2024.
