* Summary: CO Oxidation Activation Energy Experiment
:PROPERTIES:
:ARTICLE_TYPE: summary
:SOURCE_KEYS: test-notebook-entry
:STATUS: draft
:CREATED: 2026-04-03
:END:

This experiment measured the activation energy of CO oxidation
on a Pt(111) surface using temperature-programmed reaction from
300K to 600K. The measured value of 1.05 eV is consistent with
both literature values citep:jones2023 and prior DFT calculations
cite:smith2024.

** Key Findings

The Arrhenius plot was linear across the full temperature range,
suggesting a single rate-limiting step. The activation energy
agrees well with computational predictions.
